HELLO GREECE


Why Greece?

Greece is known as the cradle of western civilization. Many of our democratic ideas originated from Greece. Located in the Balkan Peninsula, Greece has breathtaking landscapes, exotic beaches, abundant forests, serene lakes, and rugged mountains. Discover a lifestyle and culture that is bound to surprise you. Greece is also home to marvelous monuments, temples, and heritage sites

Places to Visit

Santorini

Santorini is well known, and visited by thousands every year. It is one of the most beautiful and scenic in all of greece. See its alluring turquoise beaches, dine in the exquisite restaurants, and incredible sunsets and scenery.

Athens

It's impossible to visit Greece and not go to Athen; the birthplace of democracy. Athens named after the goddess Athena, is overflown with greek culture and mythology. With sites like the Parthenon, the temple of Zeus, and The Erechtheion, you can't not find things to do.
